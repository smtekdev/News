About

Launched on June. 20, 2023, the Pittsburgh Union Progress is the publication of the striking workers at the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette (and contributors including a summer intern or two).

On June. 18, 2023 Guild members at the Post-Gazette authorized an unfair labor practice strike, joining other striking workers on the picket line. The workers demand Post-Gazette management rescind the illegally imposed unilateral working conditions.
